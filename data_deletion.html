How to Delete Your Data

Your data is stored locally within the app on your device. To delete all of your data, you simply need to uninstall the app from your device.

Step 1: Find the App on Your Device

Locate the app on your device's home screen or app drawer.

Step 2: Uninstall the App

Follow the instructions below depending on your device:

  • Android: Long press the app icon and select "Uninstall" or drag it to the "Uninstall" option at the top of the screen.
  • iOS: Press and hold the app icon until it starts to wiggle, then tap the "X" icon or choose "Remove App" from the options.

Step 3: Confirm Uninstallation

Confirm that you want to uninstall the app. Once the app is uninstalled, all data stored in the local storage of the app will be permanently deleted.
