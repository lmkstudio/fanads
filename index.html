Features

FanAds app provides an easy way to view key data from your Facebook Audience Network account. It allows you to access reporting of your apps or websites, directly from your mobile device. The app provides you with apps/websites overview, as well as access to detailed performance reports on all key metrics. Available reports include: revenue, ad requests, fill rate, impressions, clicks, click rate, and eCPM.
